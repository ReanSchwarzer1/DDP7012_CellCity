Cell City

Cooperative Hidden-Role Biology Simulator

Overview

You play as cell scientists operating within a gigantic model cell, remotely piloting organelles to keep it alive. You all win together if the cell stays healthy and completes enough useful proteins before time runs out.

Beware: One player may secretly be the Virus, trying to push the cell toward collapse by misusing organelles and letting Waste build up.

Core Mechanics

1. Resource Flow

Convert Glucose into ATP, and use ATP and Amino Acids to synthesize Proteins. Watch out for Waste by-products!

2. Movement & Actions

Walk to an organelle, run its function, then move on. You are not locked to a single organelle.

3. The Waste Tower

Every action produces waste. If the Waste Tower reaches 12 discs, the cell undergoes lysis and the Virus wins.

4. Cell Scan

Use Immunity Tokens to initiate a table-wide vote to eject a suspected Virus. Be careful—wrongly accusing a healthy scientist damages the cell!

Instruction Cards

Hemoglobin

Cost: 3 AA, 1 ATP
Effect: +5 Cell Life; gain 1 ATP at the start of the next round.
Science: Carries oxygen in blood, improving energy supply.

Insulin

Cost: 2 AA, 1 ATP
Effect: +4 Cell Life; immediately give 1 Glucose to up to 2 different players.
Science: Helps cells take up glucose.

Membrane Channel Protein

Cost: 2 AA, 1 ATP
Effect: +3 Cell Life; for the rest of this round, each Grab Bag Pull may discard one Toxin for free.
Science: Channels let specific substances cross membranes safely.
